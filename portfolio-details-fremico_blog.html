Fremico Blog

Project information

  • Category: Web Development
  • Client: Personal Project

Fremico Blog was a personal project that was created to publish some news on various topics, from global news, video games and reviews. Unfortunately, I ended up closing the project due to lack of time to publish news. However, I am developing the 2nd version of the project, which will be self-sustainable and will have an autonomous publishing model without the need for human hands.
