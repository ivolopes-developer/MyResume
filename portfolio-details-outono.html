Outono

Project information

  • Category: Desktop App
  • Client: Personal Project

"Outono" is management software designed for small businesses. It was developed during my IT Systems Management course.

The software had all the basic functionalities necessary for good business management.

  • Employee Registration
  • Customer Registration
  • Supplier Registration
  • Article Registration
  • Order Registration
  • Stock Management (with choice of warehouses)
  • Generate invoice PDF
  • Simulation of sending invoices to the Portuguese finance system

  • Development environment
  • Java
  • MySQL
