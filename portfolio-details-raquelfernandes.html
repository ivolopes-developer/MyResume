Raquel Fernandes is a freelance visual identity designer. She started working as a freelancer in late 2018. Raquel contacted me to develop her personal website designed by her.

The main purpose of the website is to show to her followers and potential clients your projects in a fast, intuitive and organized way. The contact page contains a form protected by recaptcha v3 that sends information to Raquel's email in a clear and organized way.

note: The entire website is made in portuguese.
