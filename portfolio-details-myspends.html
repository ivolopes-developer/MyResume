MySpends

Project information

  • Category: Android APP
  • Client: Personal Project

"MySpends" was a project that I started developing on Android during my time at college.

The application was not very complex, it was used to manage the user's personal finances based on some inputs: "expenses" and "incomes".

When entering expenses or income, the user can choose the category of expense or income. This way, later, when the user checks his earnings/spending, he can know, with the help of a data graph, how he spent his money and where he earned it from.

I ended up abandoning the project because I started working on other opportunities that helped me support myself.


  • Development Enviornment
  • Android Studio
  • Android (Java)
