Biofisio

Project information

  • Category: Web Development
  • Client: Biofisio
  • URL: www.biofisio.pt

Biofisio contacted me at the end of 2022 because it was changing the image of the entire company with a brand designer. They had a very basic website made with Wix, and they weren't satisfied, so they told me they wanted a concise website with the company's identity.

In partnership with a story telling writer hired by Biofisio, and together with the brand designer responsible for Biofisio's new identity, we built the new website.

The website was entirely designed for Biofisio's potential customer, focused on those who do not yet know the company and mainly a portal for those who find the company through Google Search or through advertising campaigns.

To maximize the user experience, and so that users don't feel lost, I added a web chat linked to WhatsApp. This way the team can always be attentive to possible doubts from website visitors.
