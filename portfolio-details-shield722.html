Shield 722

Project information

  • Category: Web Development
  • Client: Volunteer Project
  • URL: www.shield722.github.io

For this project, I was contacted by 2 young people who played on a Roleplay server in GTA 5 FiveM. They and their group of friends took Roleplay so seriously that they approached me about developing a website for them.

As they were very young and didn't have much to pay me, I decided to do volunteer work. As Github gives the possibility of hosting a website for free, I developed and published it for them to use in their Roleplay.
