Project information

The company "Shield 722" is a fictitious company created on a GTA 5 roleplay server. It is a private security company that acts secretly for the government. Shield also acts in an illegitimate way, making loans to desperate people knowing they won't be able to pay on time with the interest applied to the amount.

note: The entire website is made in portuguese.
