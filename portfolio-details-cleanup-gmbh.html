In 2023, I began my job search process in Germany, despite not being a German speaker. I sent numerous applications to various companies where my profile matched at least 85%, intending to gain the remaining 15% through work experience.

Unfortunately, luck wasn't on my side, and I didn't even land a remote interview. I kept trying until I decided to try offering my services in exchange for work or help in finding a job.

That's when I came up with the idea of approaching CleanUP, a cleaning company where a friend of mine, also Portuguese, has been working for several years.

I reorganized all the information about CleanUP into a FigJAM file and, together with a designer, started working on remaking the website. It's now completed, and the main goal of this site isn't just to enhance my knowledge in ReactJS but also to seek help in finding employment in Germany, even if it's temporarily doing cleaning work until I secure a job in my field.
